Logistics & Freight Infrastructure

Nationwide haulage, port operations & supply chain management

Banshap's logistics division provides comprehensive freight and haulage services covering Tanzania's Northern, Central, and TAZARA corridors. From port clearance at Dar es Salaam to last-mile delivery in remote regions, we manage the complete supply chain on your behalf — with real-time cargo tracking, experienced drivers, and a fleet maintained to the highest standards.

Our logistics team holds active working relationships with the Tanzania Ports Authority, Tanzania International Container Terminal Services (TICTS), and Tanzania Ports Services Limited (TPSL) — enabling us to move goods efficiently and cost-effectively, even during peak congestion periods.

TRA & TIN Registration

Taxpayer identification, VAT registration & tax compliance setup

Following company incorporation, every business operating in Tanzania requires a Taxpayer Identification Number (TIN) from the Tanzania Revenue Authority — and, where applicable, VAT registration. We manage the full TRA registration process and set up your compliance framework from day one to ensure you start trading without regulatory risk.
